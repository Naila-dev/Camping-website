Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the common questions about our event.

When will the event take place?

The event will take place on 15th – 17th November 2025 at Mike Rowland Scouts Grounds, Nairobi.

How can I buy tickets?

You can buy tickets directly from this website under the "Buy Tickets" section. Payments can be made via M-Pesa or credit card.

Are there different ticket categories?

Yes, we offer Standard, Premium, and Pro Access tickets. Each has different benefits such as seating, access to workshops, and VIP networking sessions.

Will food and drinks be provided?

Yes, food courts and refreshment stands will be available at the venue. VIP and Pro ticket holders will also enjoy complimentary meals.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, there is secure parking space available on-site. We recommend arriving early as parking slots are limited.

Who do I contact for more information?

You can reach out to our support team via email at info@mikerowlandevents.co.ke or call us at +254 700 123 456.
