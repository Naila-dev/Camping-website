Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the informative camping retreat.

Who can attend the camping retreat?

The retreat is open to individuals of all ages who are interested in environmental education, community building, and outdoor adventure. Families, students, and professionals are all welcome.

What should I bring to the retreat?

Please bring a tent, sleeping bag, reusable water bottle, flashlight, personal hygiene items, and appropriate clothing for outdoor weather. A full checklist will be sent via email after registration.

Are meals provided during the event?

Yes, healthy and balanced meals will be provided for all participants throughout the retreat. Vegetarian and vegan options will also be available upon request.

Will there be medical assistance on site?

Yes, a certified first aid team will be present throughout the duration of the retreat to assist with any medical needs or emergencies.

Is there an age limit for participants?

There is no strict age limit, but children under 16 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. The activities are designed to be safe and enjoyable for all age groups.

How can I register for the retreat?

You can register online via our official event website. Once registered, you will receive a confirmation email with further details, including the full program and packing list.
